    It takes Gitxaala Fans to raise All Native Tournaments Champions

    Gitxaala Nation cheers, walks with and strengthens their warriors. On and Off the Court. The momentum is just as high off the court as it is on the court. The Gitxaala Nation fundraises throughout the year for their Warriors. Doing as much as possible to let the Warriors just concentrate on their game. The fundraising takes a life of it's own when it comes to our Warriors. On fundraiser averages $ 3000 to $5000 just by having the Warriors name on the advertising. Our group raised $8000 for the All Native Tournament teams. Also in 2018 we raised $ 60 000 for the Championship home coming and fan appreciation celebration in May 2018.
