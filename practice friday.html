Gitxaala Welcome

"La Gwelga Lax"

Gitxaala has the Fire burning, food is ready

Gitxaala Nation since time immemorial have the legend of being "the Sleeping Giants" meaning we are big, strong and unbeatable but soft and gentle hearts. Welcoming. When you come to the village you will be greeted at the shore with the hereditary. Calling this phrase to you. Welcoming you to the community.

Gitxaala Unity

"Saayt Gulum Goot"

Of One Heart

Gitxaala is a nation of One Heart. Our traditional teaching grooms us to be connected physically, mentally, emotionally and spiritually. In good and bad times we come together and Never leave any member alone to fend for themselves. We do not do it for them but strengthen them in their journey any way we can.
